About ANNA

The Anarchy Network Naming Authority (ANNA) serves as the neutral administrator of the .nvos.dev top-level domain, ensuring equitable access to domain registration services for all recognized sovereign entities, international organizations, and qualified applicants without discrimination based on political ideology, economic system, or territorial disputes.

Operating as an autonomous institution with strict political neutrality, ANNA maintains the technical infrastructure necessary for global domain resolution while establishing uniform standards for domain registration and management. Our commitment to transparency, technical independence, and operational security ensures the long-term stability of the root namespace.

Domain Registration Services

Application Process: ANNA evaluates applications for second-level domains under .nvos.dev according to established criteria including entity legitimacy, technical capacity, security compliance, and operational stability requirements. All applications undergo standardized security screening and technical assessment by the Administrative Authority.

Registration decisions are made with strict political neutrality, treating all qualified applicants equally regardless of governmental structure or international relations status. Complex applications requiring enhanced security review may necessitate extended evaluation periods as determined by operational requirements.
